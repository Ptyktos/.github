# Ptyktos

**Performance infrastructure for the AI age.**

Ptyktos builds high-performance APIs for the data layer that modern AI systems are built on — ingestion, extraction, transformation, and serialization — engineered for speed, throughput, and resource efficiency that legacy tooling can't approach.

A subsidiary of [TWN Group Pty Ltd](https://twn.systems).

---

## What we do

The AI era runs on data pipelines: crawling the web, extracting structure from documents, tokenizing, serializing, and moving bytes between systems. Most of that work today is slow, expensive, and wasteful at scale.

We rebuild those primitives from the ground up. Our tools are designed to be drop-in replacements for the incumbents — same job, a different order of magnitude.

Current focus areas include:

- **Acquisition** — web crawling and scraping at scale
- **Extraction** — PDF / HTML → Markdown and structured formats
- **Transformation** — tokenization and content preparation
- **Serialization** — fast, efficient data interchange

> As a representative example, our crawling and extraction layer benchmarks at **~43× faster**, **~1000× higher throughput**, and **~116× more resource-efficient** than comparable incumbent tooling on equivalent workloads.

## Why "Ptyktos"

From the Greek *πτυκτός* — *folded, able to be folded*. Compression, density, and structure brought down to their most efficient form. The same idea applied to data.

## Principles

- **Performance is the product.** Not a feature, not a tier. The baseline.
- **Drop-in, not rip-out.** Familiar interfaces, radically different internals.
- **Efficiency at scale.** Lower cost per unit of work, every layer of the stack.
- **Built to own.** Engineered in-house on infrastructure we control.
